Last Updated 22nd January 2017

Account Registration

  • General Release opens at 10am Saturday 28th January
  • Each account holder is eligible to purchase up to four tickets each

Account Photos

  • Your account must have an identifiable photo of yourself.
    • If your photo is not currently an identifiable headshot it must be changed. This can be done here.
    • An identifiable headshot must be uploaded by 28th February 2017.
    • Any changes to your photo after 28th February 2017 will be subject to an administration charge.
  • Keble Ball reserves the right to refuse you your tickets/wristbands upon collection, or refuse to post tickets/wristbands, if your account photo is not easily identifiable as you.

Ticket payment

  • Tickets must be paid for within 24 hours.
    • If tickets are not paid for they will expire and be allocated to the next person on the waiting list.
    • If your tickets expire due to non payment, we will be unable to reallocate tickets to you.
  • Tickets may be paid for by credit/debit card, or by Battels for current Keble students
    • We do not support payment by American Express (Amex) cards.
  • If you choose to pay for your tickets by card, it is imperative that you return to the Keble Ball ticketing system after completing the transaction via eWay (our payment provider).
    • If your ticket is not marked as paid, please contact the ticketing officer as soon as possible with proof of purchase (typically, the transaction receipt sent by eWay will suffice).

Group Purchase

  • Registered account holders may form a purchase group, which allows the group members' ticket allowances to be pooled.
    • To create a purchase group, follow this link.
  • The leader of a group (the person who created the group) will be a given a code that can be used by others to join the group.
    • A group may have at most 4 members, including the group leader.
    • This means that any one group can request at most 16 tickets.
  • The group leader is responsible for returning to the ticketing system when general release begins and attempting to reserve tickets for the group.
  • If successful, the tickets for the group will be assigned to each of the group members as requested.
  • It is the responsibility of each member of the group to pay for the tickets that they have requested.
  • If one member of a group does not pay for their tickets in the 24 hour period, only their tickets will expire, the rest of the groupâ€™s tickets (providing they are paid for) will still be valid

Waiting List

  • If you are unable to purchase tickets during the general release, you have the option to join the waiting list.
  • If extra tickets are released, or if other users cancel their tickets, the extra availability will be allocated to people on the waiting list.
  • Tickets will only be allocated in the blocks in which they are requested.
    • If you join the waiting list for 4 tickets, you will not be allocated tickets until there are 4 tickets available.

Cancellations, Refunds and Resales

  • Unwanted tickets may be cancelled at any time before 28th February 2017 without penalty, and you will receive a full refund.
  • Tickets may not be resold under any circumstances.
  • Any tickets found to have been resold will be cancelled, and both the seller and purchaser will be refused entry to the ball.

Guests

  • Non-Oxford University students and non-students are permitted to attend the Ball provided that they are over the age of 18 years.
  • Each guest who has had a ticket purchased on their behalf will have to register their own account on the Keble Ball website in the following weeks.

Ticket Collection

  • Once you collect your ticket, you are entirely responsible for it.
    • Keble Ball will not be able to replace or refund any lost, stolen or damaged wristbands.
  • Immediately after collection, or upon receipt of posted wristbands, you should check that you have the correct wristbands and that they are in good condition. Any mistakes can not be rectified at a later date.
    • If there is an issue with wristbands given to you at a collection point, you should talk to the members of Keble Ball staff present.
    • If there is an issue with wristbands which have been posted to you, you should contact the ticketing officer as soon as possible after receipt.
  • Keble Ball reserves the right to refuse you your tickets/wristbands upon collection, or refuse to post tickets/wristbands, if your account photo is not easily identifiable as you.
  • There will be three ticket collections preceding the Ball:
    • Wednesday 4th May 7pm - 9pm
    • Thursday 5th May 7pm - 9pm
    • Friday 6th May 7pm - 9pm
  • If you cannot attend any of these collections times, you will have to pay to have your tickets posted to you.
    • If you are a Keble Graduand with your Degree Day on 07/05/2017, your tickets will be included in your Graduation Packs.
  • Valid photo ID must be presented upon ticket collection.
    • Examples of valid ID includes University ID Cards (Bod Cards), driving licenses, passports etc.
    • Keble Ball reserves the right to refuse you your tickets/wristbands if you cannot present valid ID.
  • If you have purchased tickets for yourself as well as guests, you (the ticket owner) are eligible to collect all the tickets you ordered and paid for.
  • If you purchased tickets in a group, each group member must collect the tickets they requested and paid for.
    • The group leader is not permitted to collect tickets on behalf of the entire group
  • If you are the named guest of a ticket owner, you (the ticket holder) are eligible to collect only the ticket that is in your name.

Postage

  • Keble Ball offers postage services this year; both 1st Class and 2nd Class (non-recorded) delivery is available.
  • Ticket holders will be able to purchase postage after purchasing their tickets in the coming weeks, should they discover that they can no longer attend a collection time).
  • Tickets will be posted on Wednesday 27th April.
  • After receipt of your tickets/wristbands you are responsible for their safekeeping
    • Keble Ball will not be able to replace or refund any lost, stolen or damaged wristbands.
    • If there is an issue with wristbands which have been posted to you, you should contact the ticketing officer as soon as possible after receipt.
  • If you have not received a ticket due to be posted to you by Wednesday 4th May, please contact the ticketing officer.

On the night

  • Wristbands must be worn at all times during the Ball
    • Failure to wear your wristband will result in you being removed from the Ball.
  • You must present your wristband for inspection when asked by any member of security staff or the Keble Ball Committee
    • Failure to present your wristband when asked will result in you being removed from the Ball.
  • Valid ID is not required on the night but is strongly recommended.
